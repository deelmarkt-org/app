DeelMarkt is the trusted Dutch P2P marketplace for safe secondhand buying and selling — no scams, no stress.

🔐 ALWAYS PAY SAFELY WITH ESCROW
Your money is held in escrow until you receive and approve your item. No payment to the seller until you're satisfied. That simple.

✅ VERIFIED SELLERS VIA iDIN
Every DeelMarkt user is verified via iDIN — the Dutch banking identity standard used by ING, ABN AMRO, and Rabobank. You always know who you're dealing with.

📦 SHIP WITH QR CODE
Create a shipping label in the app, scan the QR code at a PostNL drop-off point, and your parcel is on its way. Fully trackable, integrated into the transaction.

🌟 TRUST SCORE ON EVERY PROFILE
Every buyer and seller has a transparent trust score based on completed transactions, response time, and reviews. Always choose reliable traders.

💬 SECURE CHAT WITH BUILT-IN PAYMENTS
Negotiate, ask questions, and close deals — all within the app. No sharing private details over WhatsApp or email.

🔍 SEARCH BY CATEGORY, LOCATION OR KEYWORD
From vintage clothing and electronics to furniture and sporting goods — find what you're looking for near you.
